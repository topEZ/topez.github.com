Local Area Network

LAN Design
The network is a 3-Layer network, namely Core, Distribution & Access. The Core & Distribution layer is connected through gigabit fiber optic backbone, while the access layer is using enhanced CAT5 cabling. The network is designed to provide link level and device level redundancy. The LAN Project of BITS Pilani Goa Campus is a state of the art, completely switched, voice enabled LAN infrastructure.

Bandwidth Distribution Policies
There is a network committee which has the non-CSA hostel representatives from various disciplines and hostels. Role of the committee members is to get a feedback from all hostels, sort out the technical issues and implement changes in proxy servers under the supervision of WIPRO network facility management team.






Intranet & Internet Bandwidth
The connectivity of 100 Mbps LAN include all Central Computer Lab, Faculty Cubicles, Offices, Library, Department Labs, Lecture Theatres, Workshop, Auditorium, Hostel rooms, Staff quarters, Visitors Guest house, Shopping complex, Student activity center & other public facility locations in the campus. The campus network is connected with 8 Mbps (bandwidth) link for the Internet connectivity. The Campus LAN is protected by Firewall and Intrusion Detection System for network security.

Wireless LAN Facility
A few wireless access points are available within the campus to facilitate connection of wireless network enabled laptops. These access points are located at Library, B-Dom, Auditorium & Lecture Theaters.
