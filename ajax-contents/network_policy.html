Network Policy

Information on Network and Network Usage

1. LAN Design:

The network is a 3-Layer network, namely Core, Distribution and Access. The Core and Distribution layer is connected through gigabit fiber optic backbone, while the access layer is using enhanced CAT5 cabling. The network is designed to provide link level and device level redundancy. The LAN Project of BITS Pilani K K Birla Goa Campus is a state of the art, completely switched, voice enabled LAN infrastructure.

2. Intranet and Internet Bandwidth:

The connectivity of 100 Mbps LAN include all Central Computer Lab, Faculty Cubicles, Offices, Library, Department Labs, Lecture Theatres, Workshop, Auditorium, Hostel rooms, Staff quarters, Visitors Guest house, Shopping complex, Student activity center and other public facility locations in the campus. The campus network is connected with 100 Mbps Reliance line for the Internet connectivity and 45 Mbps Tata Services as a backup. However, from the utilization point of view, 100 Mbps line supports to Hostels and 45 Mbps line supports Institute building, staff quarters and VGH. The Campus LAN is protected by Cyberoam UTM Firewall for network security

3. Wireless LAN Facility:

Wireless access points are available within the campus to facilitate connection of wireless network enabled laptops. These access points are located at Library, B-Dome, A-Wing, B-Wing, Auditorium, VGH, Computer Center, Lecture Theaters, Library and various food outlets. 6. SECURITY POLICY

All ports except those necessary for functioning of the servers are blocked. Unwanted downloads and peer to peer connections for unnecessary purposes are banned. No user has direct Access to servers. It is the user’s responsibility to protect their account from unauthorized use by changing passwords periodically and using passwords that are not easily guessed. Sharing of passwords for any purpose whatsoever is strictly prohibited.
